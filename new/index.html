Hello there! I am Hao Chen, but feel free to call me Calvin too. I hail from the beautiful Cantonese region in southeastern China. Additionally, you can find me diving deep into the realm of AI as an active explorer at the MI+x Lab in the University of Birmingham with Prof Jianbo Jiao.
